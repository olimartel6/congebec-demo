Our Story

50 Years of Cold
Chain Excellence

Founded in 1975 in Quebec, Congebec has grown from a single cold storage facility to Canada's premier temperature-controlled logistics provider. Our commitment to food safety, innovation, and client service has made us the trusted partner of leading food manufacturers and retailers across North America.

With strategic partnerships including CN Railway, we continue to expand our network and capabilities to meet the evolving needs of the Canadian food supply chain.
